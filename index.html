Bio

Hi, I'm Amanda!

I am currently the IT and Branch Supervisor for a public library in Northwest Indiana. In order to retain that position, I need to obtain a Master's degree in Library and Information Science. I started working on that in January 2024 and am specializing in Public Librarianship. I hope to graduate in Spring or Summer of 2026.

Back in December 2021, I started out as a part-time circulation clerk here at my public library. When we joined the Evergreen Indiana consortium about a year later, our transit load greatly increased, and I was asked to help our established transit person with the heavier load. We also began adding barcodes to our magazine collection at that time, so I was recruited to copy catalog those.

Now I am our tech troubleshooter and the go-between for technology needs. If I can figure it outâ€”great! If not, it's my job to contact the people and companies who can. I will eventually be training for some administrative responsibilities as well.

Before I worked at the library, I worked jobs in food service and retail. I like to stay busy, and I enjoy challenges! (Reasonable challenges, that is!)

In March 2021, I obtained my Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration from Thomas Edison State University (TESU), specializing in Entrepreneurship. I tested out of most of my required classes using CLEP, DSST (Dantes), and TECEP (TESU's test option), leaving only four classes for me to complete online. Needless to say, I am very familiar with online learning and with independent studying.

I enjoy spending time wih my family and my cat, reading books, watching old TV shows, listening to music, and finding music to add to my online radio station. I would also love to play music. I played piano a long time ago, and I'd like to get back into that. I also would like to learn guitar and drums.

It is fun to meet other library students and workers in my classes. I am excited about my professional journey ahead!
